Invader Technologies

Invader chemistry platform is a homogenous, isothermal, DNA probe-based system for highly sensitive, quantiative detection of specific nucleic acid sequences. It is an accurate and specific method for detecting single-base pair changes, insertions, deletions, gene copy number, infectious agents, gene expression. Invader reactions can be performed using genomic DNA or on amplified RNA, PCR, or RT-PCR products. The Invader process amplified a target-specific singal but not the target itself.
