Cloning/IVT Information


Some aspects of our cloning procedure:

  1. Host vector is pBluescript II SK(+) from Stratagene (AmpR, LacZ, T7/T3 promoter)
  2. Insert is PCR or RT-PCR amplified and is between 900-1500 bp in length
  3. Final construct can be linearized with validated enzymes including EcoRI, Ndel, BamHI (additional enzymes may be validated if necessary)
  4. All final constructs will be delivered with a glyercol stock
